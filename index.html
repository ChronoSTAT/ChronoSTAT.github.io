The Problem

Physical existance is hard.

Whether you suffer from a chronic illness, or just day to day problems, finding patterns in your symptoms is never easy. Sometimes medication affects your memory, or maybe you just don't feel well enough to keep track. According to the CDC , chronic illnesses affect more than half of all adults in the US, making up more than 75% of all healthcare spending. Getting an accurate diagnosis or effect treatment plan can take months, or even years, because it takes time to identify symptomatic patterns.
